Introduction to Scratch

Grades 3-5
Beginner to Intermediate
The perfect first course in programming!

Scratch is a visual language designed for kids by the MIT Media Lab.
It removes early challenges to coding such as syntax errors and typing skills by using visual, drag and drop interface.
