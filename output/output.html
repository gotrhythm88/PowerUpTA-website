Introduction to Scratch

Grades 3-5
Beginner to Intermediate
The perfect first course in programming!
Scratch is a visual language designed for kids by the MIT Media Lab. It removes early challenges to coding such as syntax errors and typing skills by using visual, drag and drop interface.

Computing ideas through play

Grades K-2
Beginner
Using iPad apps and "unplugged" activities, we will explore ideas related to computing such as sequencing, looping and decision making. Kids will also learn to create mini programs on the iPad using Scratch Jr.

IPads are provided, but if you want to keep your work, please bring your own iPad, as files are saved locally and currently cannot be shared.

Build Your Own Website

Grades 5-8
Beginner to Intermediate
Learn the languages of the internet: HTML, CSS and Javascript. Kids will build a multi-page website on a topic of their choice and learn to add text, headings, links, images and embedded content such as videos. We will also cover styling tools to make pages have any look and feel they can imagine.
